NAME

santosh — opens black boxes: neural nets, version control, whatever's opaque

SYNOPSIS

santosh [--open-boxes] [--local-first] [--interpret <model>] [--ship] [--no-boring]

DESCRIPTION

santosh opens black boxes — neural nets, version control, whatever's opaque. The operating thesis: the interesting work is always inside the box everyone else treats as sealed. CS undergrad at PES University, Bengaluru; incoming research intern at PESU Intelligence Labs (interpretability / why models hallucinate). Open to SWE / ML internships · Summer 2026.

OPTIONS
--rebuild
if I can't rebuild it, I don't understand it.
--local-first
your data, your GPU, no server.
--why-not-whether
whether it works is easy; why it fails is the work.
--no-boring
boring is the only thing I refuse to ship.
EXAMPLES
$ santosh --ml --in-browser
# neural net, backprop by hand, no framework — ~97% MNIST (Gradient)
$ santosh --projects
# GlassBox · Gradient · Orbit — each one gets a proper page →
