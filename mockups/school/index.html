School
— 5 Ways

Five interactive treatments of the education section, all in one neo-brutalist language — red, white, black, hard shadows, mono type, no soft edges. Each turns the same facts — PES University, B.Tech CSE, Sep 2024 – May 2028, CGPA 7.95/10 — into something quietly alive. Pick a card to open the live mockup. Each is a single self-contained HTML file.

01 OPEN →
Live Degree Timeline

A horizontal track of 8 semester ticks with a block marker pinned to today — computed live from the date, so it advances on its own and pulses at Sem 4. ■■■■□□□□ mono readout; achievement stars pinned to the semesters they happened in. Hover a tick to see what went down there.

02 OPEN →
Timeline As Scroll

The degree becomes the scrollbar. Scrub the marker from Sep 2024 to today; coursework chips stamp in and achievement stars punch in at the scroll point matching their semester. Hard, snappy reveals — no soft fades.

scroll-scrub stamp-in gsap
03 OPEN →
CGPA Hard Meter

The 7.95 earns itself: count 0.00 → 7.95 in mono while a 10-segment block bar stamps in — discrete, no gradient — and a tiny hard bar chart of per-semester SGPA draws in beside it. The number becomes a story.

count-up segment bar sgpa chart
04 OPEN →
Stamps + Live Timeline

The ★ honors hit the page like rubber stamps — scale-punch, rotation, a hard thunk, faint ink texture — sitting above the live degree timeline from 01: 8 semester ticks, pinned stars, hover popovers, and a today-marker computed from the real date. Hit RE-STAMP to replay.

05 OPEN →
Dot-Matrix Transcript

The card prints itself line-by-line like a receipt, print-head sweep and all. A [ VIEW TRANSCRIPT ] toggle hard-expands the card — shadow growing as it pushes open — into a raw semester-by-semester data table. Registrar office meets brutalism.
