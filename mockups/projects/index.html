Living Instruments

Each black hatched header runs a tiny 2-colour canvas of the project itself — ORBIT slots energy blocks onto a curve, GLASSBOX lights an attention matrix, GRADIENT trains a net live, PES-VCS draws its commit graph. Cards lift off their shadow on hover and grow into the case study on click. LIVE badges ping real deployments; stats count up; the filter FLIP-sorts the grid.
