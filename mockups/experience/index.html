Treatments of the experience section in one neo-brutalist language — red, cream, ink, hard stacked shadows, mono type, no soft edges. The headline idea: the roster is a phone, and the phone means something. Contacts become a call log; current roles ring in as genuine incoming calls; the answered card is a vCard. The Switchboard

Click a contact and it dials — the avatar pulses, a CONNECTING ticker runs, it rings, then answers into the detail. Current roles ring as a full-bleed incoming call with hard DECLINE / ANSWER. A CONTACTS / RECENTS toggle turns the roster into a chronological call log (incoming / outgoing / missed glyphs), and flipping the view FLIP-animates the rows. Live roles pulse ● ACTIVE with a duration counting up; ★ becomes blocky signal bars. Swipe a row to reveal actions, scrub the A–Z rail, or speed-dial from the keypad. The answered card flips to a vCard with a brutalist QR; the blurb types out under a waveform like a voicemail.
