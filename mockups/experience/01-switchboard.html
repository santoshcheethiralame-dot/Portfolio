The Switchboard

The roster, rebuilt as a phone that means something. Contacts become a call log — incoming/outgoing/missed glyphs are your career timeline. Current roles ring as genuine incoming calls you answer; the rest dial out. Live roles pulse and count up; reception bars stand in for impact; the answered card flips to a vCard. Pure neo-brutalism — ink, cream, red, mono.
